Key Concepts

The Challenge: Shipping is a conservative, old-school industry. Owners reluctant to adopt new tech. Regulations evolving. Huge opportunity for innovation.
Fuel Quality:

Ships use different fuel grades. Manual switching between fuel types. Opportunity for automation.

Scrubber Tech:

Manually operated equipment to reduce emissions. Regulation-dependent (varies by port). Carbon Ridge alternative in development.

Legislation:

OCCS (operational carbon cutting systems) still being defined. Industry working groups shaping future regulations.

Deployment Timeline:

Q1 2028 first commercial deployment. Customers don't need to see it before ordering. VCs unfamiliar with maritime timelines.

Company Advantage

• Proprietary hardware (hard to replicate)

• Lean team, critical AI integration
• Team experience: on-water logistics, shipbroking, VCs
