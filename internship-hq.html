Key Concepts

The Challenge

Shipping is old-school and conservative. Owners reluctant to adopt new tech. Regulations evolving. Huge innovation opportunity.

Fuel Quality

Ships use different fuel grades. Manual switching between types. Opportunity for automation.

Scrubber Tech

Manually operated equipment to reduce emissions. Regulation-dependent (varies by port). Carbon Ridge alternative in development.

Legislation

OCCS (operational carbon cutting systems) still being defined. Industry working groups shaping regulations.
